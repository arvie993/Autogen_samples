### Enhanced Features for **Travel Wallet**:

1. **Gamified Savings Challenges:**
   - Introduce challenges where users can set savings goals for their upcoming trips. Completing challenges could earn them badges or points, which could be redeemed for discounts on travel-related products or experiences. This makes budgeting fun and engaging!

2. **Personalized Travel Itineraries with Financial Insights:**
   - Incorporate an itinerary builder that not only suggests activities but also displays expected costs alongside each suggestion. Users can see how each activity affects their budget in real-time, making planning more transparent and less stressful.

3. **Collaborative Travel Budgets:**
   - Allow users to create and manage group budgets for trips with friends or family. Users can see shared expenses and track contributions, fostering collaboration and preventing financial misunderstandings before they arise.

4. **Augmented Reality (AR) Expense Tracking:**
   - Utilize AR to let users scan their receipts with their phone camera. The app would automatically categorize and log expenses, reducing manual entry and making it feel like a modern, futuristic experience.

5. **Local Currency Alert System:**
   - Users can set alerts for favorable exchange rates or when local currencies drop, allowing them to make informed decisions about when to convert their money, adding an element of financial savvy to their travels.

6. **Virtual Financial Advisors:**
   - Introduce an AI-driven chatbot that answers financial queries related to travel—things like “What’s the best way to tip in Japan?” or “How do I avoid foreign transaction fees?” This personal assistant can empower users with knowledge right when they need it.

7. **Cultural Experiences & Local Deals:**
   - Partner with local businesses to offer users exclusive deals or experiences that align with their interests and budget, enhancing their cultural immersion and supporting local economies.

8. **Sustainability Dashboard:**
   - Provide insights into how spending habits affect the environment. For instance, suggest eco-friendly alternatives for travel activities or track carbon footprints based on spending patterns—adding a layer of social responsibility to their travel choices.

### Community Engagement:
